Dearest Sarah,

How have you been since the last time we spoke?

A Courteous Check-In

I hope this message finds you enveloped in warmth and peace. It’s been a little while since we last talked, and I often find myself wondering how you are doing.

I hope your days have been gentle and that you are smiling brightly, just as beautifully as I remember.

Shared Energy & Understanding

I want you to understand that the fact I also went silent doesn't mean I gave up on you or stopped caring. I am also a plant that needs to be watered, just as much as I water others.

You know how it feels to pour energy out and not receive the same in return. I am human too, and being treated coldly hurts. It brings me peace when you consider my feelings alongside yours.

Writing this to you comes from a place of great honor and from the depth of the friendship we had. If I were viewing this merely from the angle of lovers, the anger of being hurt would have clouded my words. We were smiling together, and then suddenly, you gave up.

You left without even closing the door, as if the house that contained you was no longer of importance. I didn't deserve that. But in all that, my maturity and the bond we shared dissolves those feelings, and that's why I am able to keep pushing forward in a healthy way.

Respecting Your Journey

You told me you wanted to take a step back to find yourself, discover your own peace, and grow in the spirit. I want you to know how deeply I respect and admire that decision. I don't want you to ever feel guilty for choosing yourself and your spiritual path. Growth is irresistible and absolutely necessary.

I want you to know that choosing you, over and over again, isn't born out of desperation or weakness. It's the hallmark of a true man who stands by his words, his feelings, and his decisions. I care for you deeply, and I respect your space to flourish.

A Little Laughter

I was thinking about us and a short funny memory crossed my mind... remember all those inside jokes? Or how we used to literally talk like our parents giving advice? It made me smile so hard my cheeks hurt.

Actually, speaking of making me smile, I have a few questions for you:

"Are you a Wi-Fi signal? Because despite the distance, I’m still feeling a strong connection. 📶"
"Do you have a map? Because I keep getting lost in your eyes... and apparently, I get lost trying to figure out how to cook without burning things, so I really need you back! 🗺️"

I genuinely miss making jokes and having fun with you, physically and over the phone. I miss dancing for you. Please, never feel like you were a burden or that you did something bad. You brought light.

The Value of You

Even if a love affair isn't where we are right now, Sarah, you are so much more important than just a romantic title. I genuinely miss your advice, our deep talks, and the time we spent together. Every moment was worth it because your presence brings a profound light into my life.

Faith & Focus

I took my time before I chose you, and that is why I am not giving up on you. The love we share found us when we were already happy, and we shouldn't let it just fly away.

I believe in myself, and I know I have exactly what it takes to take care of the woman I cherish. Despite this heartbreaking moment, I am constantly praying for you.

No one is rushing things. I, too, am focusing on the things I want to achieve as a man, and I know God is going to help us both achieve greatness. The perfections I see in you—they are real, and the vision of them is beautifully doable.
